Nakoda Language App

The langauge app was designed to accompany the K-5 Nakoda language curriculum. Our linguist wrote simplified versions of traditional stories. Through the app children will be able to listen to the stories that they are learning in school. We are exploring the idea of using Nakota sight words to help to foster literacy and aid reading comprehension. I created the UI design and app icons, many of which use artwork created by Chelysa Cyr and other native artists--their work brings all of our materials to life.
